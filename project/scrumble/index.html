Scrumble

Global Game Jam.

2012 Character Design

Scrumble is an iPad platformer game created in 48 hours during 2012 Global Game Jam in Vancouver, BC.

The theme that year was “Ouroboros”. Traverse levels as Mr. Sporington. Regenerate the ever-crumbling world by spreading spores before they disintegrate beneath your feet.

I helped to develop the story and visuals. Assets created include the sprite for Mr. Sporington, the main character, foreground elements and title type. Our team of six created background art, platforms, shading, particle effects, music, programming and level design. Much coffee was involved!
